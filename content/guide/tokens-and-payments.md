# Tokens & Payments

**Last updated: [19/05/2026]**


Tokens gate one thing on DealCollab: **reaching a specific counterparty.**
Everything else is free.

## What is always free

- Submitting a mandate — no cap on active mandates.
- Being matched by the engine.
- Receiving match notifications.
- Viewing match cards on your Deal Dashboard.
- Receiving an EOI from another user.
- Approving or declining an incoming EOI.

There is no subscription, no licence fee, no success fee, and no brokerage.
You do not need to buy tokens to participate.

## What costs tokens

**Sending an Expression of Interest (EOI): 50 tokens.**

- Tokens are deducted **only when the counterparty approves your EOI.**
- If they decline or do not respond, you pay nothing.
- On approval, you receive the counterparty's verified contact — advisor name
  and phone number — and a live thread opens on your Deal Dashboard.

## The three rules to remember

1. Your mandate participation is free.
2. Identity stays sealed until both sides agree.
3. You pay only when a counterparty engages.

## Your token ledger

Every credit, debit, and top-up is logged with the action, amount, and your
balance after the transaction. Visible under **Profile → Billing**. If you
believe a deduction is wrong, contact [support email] within 7 days.

## Running out of tokens

If your balance is too low to send a new EOI, nothing else is affected: your
mandates stay active, matching continues, and you can still receive, approve,
and decline incoming EOIs. Top up under **Profile → Billing**.

## Refunds

Tokens are deducted only on approval, so there is nothing to refund for
declines or silence. Once deducted, tokens are **non-refundable** — they pay
for the confidential introduction, not the outcome of any conversation or
deal. Unused token balances: [DECIDE: refundable on account closure, or not —
state it explicitly either way].

## What tokens are not

Tokens are a prepaid platform credit. They are not money, a deposit, an
investment, or a security, and they carry no interest or resale value.

## Pricing changes

We may change token pricing for **future** actions with advance notice in-app.
Changes never apply retroactively to EOIs already sent.
