# FAQ

**Last updated: [19/5/2026]**

## About the platform

**What is DealCollab?**
Discovery infrastructure for mid-market M&A and strategic transactions.
Professionals submit mandates; the engine surfaces aligned counterparties
based on intent, sector, deal size, geography, structure, and financial
scale. We are not a broker, advisor, or representative in any transaction.

**What transactions does it support?**
Majority and minority M&A, growth equity, strategic acquisitions, asset
sales, joint ventures, fundraising, debt, and structured partnerships —
domestic and cross-border.

**What makes this different from other M&A networks?**
Most networks rely on manual circulation or broad distribution. DealCollab is
an intelligence layer, not a listing portal: confidentiality-first, no
broadcasting of your mandate, matching based on structured alignment rather
than volume.

**Will it replace the network I built over years?**
No. It's a probability enhancer, not a replacement — it surfaces
counterparties beyond your immediate circle while your relationships remain
yours.

**Why hire this instead of an analyst?**
You can do both. An analyst works linearly through their network and
bandwidth; the platform evaluates your mandate against every active mandate
continuously. It extends your reach, it doesn't replace your judgment.

## Pricing

**What does it cost?**
Submitting mandates, being matched, viewing match cards, and receiving,
approving, or declining EOIs are all free. The only paid action is sending an
Expression of Interest to a specific counterparty: **50 tokens, deducted only
if they approve.** No subscription, no licence fee, no success fee, no
brokerage. Full details: Tokens & Payments.

**Do you charge success fees if a deal closes?**
No. We don't charge for outcomes, on or off the platform.

**Is there a lock-in or long-term contract?**
No. Participate or pause at your discretion.

## Mandates & matching

**How many mandates can I upload?**
No fixed cap. Each is processed and matched independently.

**Is there a required format?**
No — chat through the intake, or paste a teaser/brief and confirm the
extracted summary. See "Get Matched Faster" for how to do it in one message.

**How long until I get a match?**
Matching is relevance-based, not time-based. If aligned counterparties exist
in the network, matches surface quickly; if not, your mandate stays active
and is re-evaluated as new mandates enter. We don't promise timelines — see
"What We Do and Don't."

**What parameters drive matching?**
Strategic and semantic alignment of intent, sector and sub-sector relevance,
deal size compatibility, transaction structure, geography, and financial
scale. Every match includes the rationale for why it surfaced, so you judge
it before engaging.

**Can I refine my mandate after upload?**
Yes, anytime. Edits flow into matching immediately, and the engine
re-evaluates alignment.

**What if the AI misunderstands my mandate?**
Edit it — updated inputs immediately drive future matching. The match
rationale is visible, so misalignment is easy to spot and correct.

**How do I hear about matches?**
On your Deal Dashboard, in-app. Email and WhatsApp notifications are planned
but not yet live.

**Do you filter out fake or low-quality proposals?**
Yes. Mandates are evaluated for clarity and consistency; thin or inconsistent
proposals are deprioritized, and advisors are KYC-verified. Note the limit:
we verify identity, not the business claims in any mandate — diligence
remains yours.

**What if multiple advisors upload the same deal?**
The system can flag similarity internally, but ownership and exclusivity stay
between advisors and their clients. We remain neutral infrastructure.

## Confidentiality & data

**Is my data secure?**
Sensitive fields are encrypted, identities stay anonymised until EOI
approval, access to confidential data is role-restricted and logged, and we
never sell mandate data. Details: Privacy & Data.

**When is my identity revealed?**
Only when an EOI between you and a counterparty is approved. Until then, both
sides see anonymised parameters and rationale only.

**What happens if there's a breach?**
Affected users are notified promptly, access is restricted immediately, and
we act in line with the DPDP Act, including notification to the Data
Protection Board where required.

**Can I permanently delete my proposal?**
Yes, anytime. It leaves matching immediately and is removed from operational
systems per our retention policy.

**What's your real incentive — subscriptions or data?**
Token revenue from successful introductions. We do not sell, aggregate, or
commercialize confidential mandate data.

## Governance

**What is your legal existence?**
DealCollab is operated by Dealzebra Global Intelligence LLP, incorporated in
India.

**Are you regulated?**
We provide discovery infrastructure only — no brokerage, advisory, valuation,
or investment services — and therefore do not operate as a regulated
financial intermediary. For jurisdiction-specific questions, contact us.

**Is DealCollab neutral between buyers and sellers?**
Fully. Matching runs on alignment criteria, not commercial bias, and we take
no position in any transaction.

**Is there a reputation system?**
No public ratings currently. Internal signals (mandate clarity, engagement
quality, responsiveness) maintain seriousness on the network.
